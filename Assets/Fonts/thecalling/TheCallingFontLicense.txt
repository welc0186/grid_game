Based on the alien (code language) displayed in TheFatRat's video for his song "The Calling" 
(https://youtu.be/KR-eV7fHNbM). 
As an official version wasn't released (as of this date) I decided to make it myself! 
Some letters (j, q, z and special characters) were not included in the video so I added them, 
trying to stay as close to the theme of the other characters as possible. Free for any use, have fun!
